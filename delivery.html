Delivery Information

We ensure your cakes are delivered fresh, on time, and in perfect condition. Below is everything you need to know about our delivery process.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver within selected regions to guarantee freshness and quality.

  • Nairobi and surrounding areas
  • Same-day delivery within city limits
  • Extended delivery upon prior arrangement

Delivery Time

Orders are processed once confirmed and scheduled based on location and cake type.

  • Standard delivery: 2–4 hours
  • Custom cakes: As per agreed schedule
  • Peak days may require extra time

Delivery Charges

Delivery fees vary depending on distance and order size.

  • Within CBD: From KSh 300
  • Outside CBD: Distance-based pricing
  • Special event deliveries quoted separately

Handling & Packaging

All cakes are securely packaged to maintain shape, freshness, and hygiene during transit.

  • Sturdy cake boxes
  • Temperature-conscious handling
  • Care instructions provided

Please ensure someone is available to receive the order at the delivery location. SweetTooth is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect address details or unavailability of the recipient.
